Protected constructor to enforce usage of shared pointer.

Parameters
reconstructionShared pointer to the Reconstruction that the Policy suggests new views for.
n_viewsNumber of accepted views this Policy is required to generate before it considers the Reconstruction complete.
uniformFlag, if true then the this Policy starts be generating n_views worth of ordered, uniformly sampled views. If false, or one more than v_views have been generated in the uniform manner, the Policy samples the spherical surface randomly.
unorderedIf this is true and uniform is true, then the Policy will return uniformly sampled points but visits them in an unordered manner. A Sphere Policy with uniform and unordered sampling flags is pseudo low-discrepancy.
radiusMinimum radius for Camera poses.
radius_maxMaximum radius for Camera poses, used for randomly sampled views only.
seedSeed for the random generator the Policy uses. Default -1 for a random seed.
